garri

Description

eye watering and tantalizing, very easy to make as well

can be enjoyed with any meal

Ingredients

  • cassava flakes
  • sugar
  • ice block
  • water - cold preferably

Steps

  1. get a bowl
  2. pour the dry flakes into it
  3. add sugar
  4. add water
  5. add ice blocks if you want
  6. meal is done!

parfait

Description

eye watering and tantalizing, very easy to make as well

can be enjoyed with any meal

Ingredients

  • fruits
  • yoghurt
  • coconut shavings

Steps

  1. get a bowl
  2. pour the yoghurt into it
  3. add coconut shavings
  4. add fruits
  5. preserve in fridge
  6. meal is done!
